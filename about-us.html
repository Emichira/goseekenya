GoSeeKenya

Welcome to GoSeeKenya! We are a dedicated team of travel enthusiasts, passionate about showcasing the beauty and diversity of Kenya to the world. Our mission is to provide authentic, immersive and unforgettable experiences to all our clients.

We offer a wide range of tailor-made safari packages to suit different interests, budgets, and preferences. Our safaris cover the most popular destinations in Kenya, including the Masai Mara, Amboseli National Park, Tsavo National Park, Samburu National Reserve, Lake Nakuru National Park, and many others. Whether you're interested in wildlife, culture, adventure, or relaxation, we have the perfect safari for you.

Our experienced and knowledgeable guides are passionate about wildlife and conservation, and will provide you with a wealth of information and insights about Kenya's flora and fauna, as well as the local cultures and traditions. We use comfortable and well-maintained vehicles for our safaris, and offer a range of accommodation options, from luxurious lodges to budget-friendly campsites.

At GoSeeKenya, we are committed to responsible tourism and sustainable development. We also adhere to the highest safety and ethical standards, to ensure that our clients have a safe, enjoyable and responsible safari experience.

Thank you for considering GoSeeKenya for your safari adventure. We look forward to showing you the best that Kenya has to offer!

Mission Statement

To provide our customers with exceptional safari experiences that promote conservation, education, and cultural understanding, while maintaining the highest standards of safety, professionalism, and customer service.

Vision Statement

To be the leading safari company in Africa, recognized for our commitment to responsible tourism, sustainability, and community development.

Our Culture:

Our company culture is built on a foundation of respect, integrity, and teamwork. We believe in creating a positive work environment that encourages innovation, creativity, and personal growth. We also strive to promote diversity, equity, and inclusion in all aspects of our business.

Our Goals:

Our primary goal is to provide our customers with unforgettable safari experiences that exceed their expectations. We also aim to contribute to the conservation and protection of the natural habitats and wildlife of the areas in which we operate. Additionally, we are committed to supporting the development of local communities by promoting sustainable tourism practices and creating opportunities for economic growth and empowerment.

Core Values:

  • Excellence - We are committed to providing the highest standards of service and professionalism in everything we do.
  • Sustainability - We believe in responsible tourism practices that support the conservation and protection of natural environments and wildlife.
  • Cultural Understanding - We strive to promote cultural understanding and respect for local communities and their traditions.
  • Safety - We prioritize the safety and security of our customers and employees at all times
  • Community Development - We are committed to creating positive impacts and supporting the development of local communities through sustainable tourism practices.
