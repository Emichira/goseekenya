Attractions Around Nairobi, Kenya: For the Solitude Travelers

  • 12 Apr 2023
  • 5 min read

As a solitude traveler, you may be looking for serene and tranquil attractions around Nairobi, Kenya that offer a sense of peace and solitude. Here are some suggestions:

  1. Nairobi National Park: Located just outside the bustling city, Nairobi National Park is a unique wildlife sanctuary where you can spot animals such as lions, giraffes, rhinos, and buffalo against the backdrop of Nairobi's skyline. You can take a guided safari tour or opt for a self-drive safari to explore the park at your own pace.
  2. Karura Forest: This lush green forest located in the heart of Nairobi is a perfect place for solitude seekers. It offers various nature trails for hiking, walking, or cycling, where you can immerse yourself in the serene beauty of the forest. There are also picnic spots, caves, and waterfalls to explore within the forest.
  3. Ngong Hills: If you're a nature lover and enjoy hiking, Ngong Hills is a great place to escape to. These beautiful hills offer panoramic views of Nairobi and the surrounding landscape. You can hike along the trails, enjoy a picnic, or simply relax and take in the breathtaking views.
  4. David Sheldrick Wildlife Trust: This renowned elephant orphanage located in Nairobi is a haven for baby elephants and rhinos that have been rescued from the wild. You can visit the trust during the daily feeding sessions, learn about their conservation efforts, and get up close and personal with these gentle giants.
  5. Nairobi Arboretum: If you're a fan of flora and fauna, Nairobi Arboretum is a peaceful oasis within the city. This 30-hectare park is home to a wide variety of indigenous trees, plants, and bird species. You can take a leisurely walk or have a picnic in the serene surroundings.
  6. Karen Blixen Museum: This historic museum is located in the former home of Danish author Karen Blixen, who wrote the famous book "Out of Africa". It offers a glimpse into the life and times of Karen Blixen and the colonial history of Kenya. The museum is set in beautiful gardens that offer a tranquil atmosphere for a solitary stroll.

These are just a few of the attractions around Nairobi, Kenya that offer solitude and tranquility for the solitude traveler. Kenya is a country rich in natural beauty and cultural heritage, and there are many more hidden gems waiting to be discovered. Remember to always respect the environment, wildlife, and local communities when visiting these attractions. Happy travels!
